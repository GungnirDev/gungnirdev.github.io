ABOUT ME


I made an "about me" page but I never know what to say about myself.

Time to try I guess :P

I'm just another bored geek with too much time on his hands and a huge love for tech. Born in Ponta Delgada, Azores in 1997, I grew up fascinated with technology (especially pre-2000 video games). I started programming my first video games at the age of 11 using Game Maker, and even though I've never finished any projects, it's still my most passionate hobby.

Right now I play mostly League of Legends and World of Warcraft (catch me in Warmane WOTLK) and I played SMITE dilligently for 8 years, I quit eventually because HiRez can suck my left nut. I also stopped playing Valorant because the hamster that powers my PC through a tiny wheel has been recently diagnosed with osteoporosis.

I'm also very much into anime / manga. It's very difficult to say what my favorite anime shows are, since they're so many and my top picks change according to the mood, but if I had to choose I'd say : Neon Genesis Evangelion, Serial Experiments Lain, Cowboy Bebop and Dragon Ball. As for manga, I don't really read manga that often, but when I do it's almost always something by Junji Ito or Boku no Hero Academia. Ibitsu is also a fantastic little hidden gem for those who are into horror manga. :)
Ironically enough, in my reading habits outside of manga, I am simply incapable of reading anything that isn't non-fiction. I'll find myself losing focus or growing bored in the first page. I do however, love reading scientific books, biographies or anything related to those two. Isaac Asimov (at least his non-fiction books) are my top picks for literature.

Oh, and I also make music. In case you want to check it out, here's a link to my SoundCloud, which is also in my Links page.
