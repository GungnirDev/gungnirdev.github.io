The Primordial Post - Dec 31 22, 09:45 GMT-1

Today The Undercon is born out of my ennui and overall lack of ideas for other projects. After delving into the Lain community for some time, I stumbled across some very interesting and obscure websites with perplexing aesthetics and designs crafted by very talented individuals. Thus, I decided to make my own internet corner, probably something that will probably never be seen, but I digress, it's my own special little corner.
If you somehow stumbled your way into here, congratulations, your rocky road led you nowhere interesting, but you're stil welcome nevertheless.

My next goal with this project is to, somehow, find a way to insert new blog entries automatically directly through automated bash scripts without having to directly add them by editing the HTML file. It's most likely an easy task, but I'm most certainly going to make it more complicated than it has to be, just like it's always been. But as long as it works and I enjoy the journey, all is well.
