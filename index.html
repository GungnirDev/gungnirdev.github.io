Dreamcast is now working - Jan 16 23, 11:27 GMT-1

At long last I got my Sega Dreamcast to work. After being plagued with the same issue with three consoles in a row (the issue being it's unable to spin the disc, therefore not reading it), all with console batches from Indonesia, I realized the disc would spin with the lid almost closed.

I thought that maybe the issue could perhaps lie in the lid switch, and it was. I was able to get one of them to work by taping the switch down inside the console, but sadly the other one wasn't lucky enough to be saved. It's still a 50% success rate :3
I later realized the lid mechanism was touching the duct taped switch, causing it to stop working again, so I cut the switch in half with a pair of scissors. The fix appears to be permanent.

- Cornflake
